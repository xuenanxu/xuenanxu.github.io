Background

The literature research result shows that dehydration impairs people's physical and mental performances, while most of the people are not drinking enough. 75% of U.S. citizens are dehydrated. The average daily water intake is 1.8 cups, which is significantly lower than the recommended daily water intake of 8.5 cups by the European hydration institute. We targeted this problem for the severity of the current dehydration situation.

Overview

Water Fortune is a mobile app that encourages people to form the habit of drinking enough water and keeping themselves hydrated by the 4 key features below.

01. Onboarding

Users get to know the key features of Water Fortune by going through the onboarding screens

02. Customizable Goal Setting

Users started by setting up a customizable goal based on their physicality, during which they are able to customize their own bottle size, measure the water intake with their own bottle size and be reminded of the unreasonable goal.

03. Water Intake Logging

Throughout the day, users log in their water intake for every bottle of water they drink. They also get to know the some water-drinking tips while realizing their drinking goal. The water intake logging will unveil a fortune cookie note.

04. Water Intake Record

Users could view their performance on drinking water on the record page and modify the physicality basics if needed.

05. Intelligent Reminder

The reminder doesn't bother users at the unnecessary time, but remind users of drinking water at the right time.
